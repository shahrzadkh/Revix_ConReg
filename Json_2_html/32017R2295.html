<ol class="crrNumList"> <li>Institutions shall disclose the amount of encumbered and unencumbered assets under the applicable accounting framework by asset type in columns C010, C040, C060, and C090 of Template A of Annex I, in accordance with the instructions in Annex II.</li> <li>Institutions shall disclose information on collateral received by asset type in columns C010 and C040 of Template B of Annex I, in accordance with the instructions in Annex II.</li> <li>Institutions shall disclose the liabilities associated with encumbered assets and collateral received as set out in Template C of Annex I, in accordance with the instructions in Annex II.</li> <li>Institutions shall disclose narrative information relating to the impact of their business model on their level of encumbrance and the importance of encumbrance in their business model as set out in Template D of Annex I, in accordance with the instructions in Annex II.</li> </ol>Disclosure requirements for all institutionsArticle 1
