<div class="crrArticle"> <p>For the purposes of this Regulation the following definitions apply:</p> <ol class="crrNumList"> <li>Internalised settlement instruction means an instruction by a client of the settlement internaliser to place at the disposal of the recipient an amount of money or to transfer the title to, or interest in, a security or securities by means of a book entry on a register, or otherwise, which is settled by the settlement internaliser in its own books and not through a securities settlement system.</li> <li>Failed internalised settlement instruction means non-occurrence of settlement, or partial settlement, of a securities transaction at the date agreed by the parties concerned due to a lack of securities or cash, regardless of the underlying cause.</li> </ol> </div>Article 1
